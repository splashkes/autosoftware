# Brief

## Core Goal
Encourage participation and strengthen community by preserving and surfacing competition data across time and organizations.

## Problem
Flower show data is:
- Fragmented across clubs
- Poorly structured (PDFs, paper)
- Not discoverable
- Not preserved over time

## Users
- Clubs / Organizations
- Judges
- Entrants
- General public

## Desired Outcomes
- Easy ingestion of competition data
- Rich public browsing experience
- Cross-show and cross-organization insights
- Recognition via awards and leaderboards

## Non-Goals (v1)
- Full admin UI for data entry
- Complex moderation workflows
- Historical data editing (append-only preferred)
