# Brief

## Core Goal
Encourage participation and strengthen community by preserving and surfacing competition data across time and organizations.

## Problem
Flower show data is:
- Fragmented across clubs
- Poorly structured (PDFs, paper)
- Not discoverable
- Not preserved over time

Additionally:
- Local schedules depend on external governing rulebooks (OJES, Publication 34) but the link is implicit
- PDFs are not just data sources — they are legal and judging references that must be cited
- Show operations (judging, scoring, media capture) lack real-time tooling
- Scoring is placement-based with no rubric provenance

## Users
- Clubs / Organizations (show hosts, admins)
- Judges
- Entrants
- General public

## Desired Outcomes
- Easy ingestion of competition data with source provenance
- Rich public browsing experience with schedule and taxonomy navigation
- Cross-show and cross-organization insights
- Recognition via awards and leaderboards
- Show Admin control panel for live show-night operations
- Standards and edition tracking for governing rulebooks
- Rubric-based scoring with audit trail

## Non-Goals (v1)
- Complex moderation workflows
- Historical data editing (append-only preferred)
- Cross-organization federation analytics
